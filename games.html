Math Hero

Game: Math Hero is inspired by Guitar Hero, only this time you can learn algebra.
Controls: Press the correct number on the keypad Developer: Pro Ant Games

Dandelion Hunt

Game: Dandelions are beautiful and immensely irritating as they pop up everywhere. Kids love them, grown ups want to get rid of them. In this game, you need to pick as many as you can before the time runs out.
Controls: Click or tap on the dandelions to make them go away. So much easier than reality! Developer: Pro Ant Games

Spaceship Duel

Game: Spaceship Duel is a simple game that you can play against friends or against the most intelligent AI in the universe. May the best laser-argument win!
Controls: Player 1 and singleplayer: Arrows right and left to move left and right, spacebar to shoot. Player 2: A to move left, D to move right, Q to shoot.
Developer: Pro Ant Games.

War Card Game

Game: The classic card game "War". A card from each deck is picked randomly, and the player with the highest rated card wins. Totally random. Could be an outset for a combat system. I really want to make it more sexy with sounds and animations, but haven't pulled myself together to do it yet.
Controls: Click the "Play" button and see whether Player 1 or 2 wins.
Developer: Pro Ant Games.

Pointless Space Shooter

Game: Pointless Space Shooter is inspired by all the good and great arcade shooters from the 80's and 90's. IMPORTANT: Play in full screen to see everything.
Controls: Use arrows to move around. Use "C" on the keyboard to shoot.
Developer: Pro Ant Games.

Arcade Fire Button Challenge

Game: Arcade Fire Button Challenge is a clicker - and that's it! Click like a maniac and get rewarded with animated gifs.
Controls: Click or tap. Even animals can play this game.
